The Homey Unifi Protect App integrates your Ubiquiti Unifi Protect system with Homey, enabling you to monitor and control your Unifi cameras directly from your Homey environment. With this app, you can:

* View camera images within Homey.
* Trigger Homey flows based on motion detection or other camera events.
* Access snapshots and camera status for automation and security purposes.
* This integration helps you create a smarter, more secure home by combining the power of Unifi Protect with Homey’s automation capabilities.
